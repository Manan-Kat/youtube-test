Skywalk

The skywalk of Thapar is an open area known for its scenic beauty. It comprises a library, lecture theatres, and a science faculty. Students find it a very relaxing place to hang out with their friends and enjoy the company of their peers.

Main Auditorium

The main auditorium is the largest auditorium at Thapar university having a capacity of 450 students. It is fully air-conditioned and has comfortable chairs. Many social events and large seminars are held here.
