Food joints.

Jaggi

The OG hangout spot of the campus. It houses a number of outlets ranging from juice stalls, confectionary shops to sandich corner. The east of Jaggi is flanked by the massive Main cafeteria of the institute, which is managed by Sodexo corporation.

G Block

Just beside the academic centre of 'G Block', this hangout spot goes by the same name. Iqbal Juice, G Block cafeteria, and the numerous snack shops on the street are just some of the things that make G block the go-to place for snacking that is light on the pocket!
